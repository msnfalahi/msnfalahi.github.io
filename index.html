About Me

As a Mechanical Engineering graduate student at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST), I specialize in Energy Conversion with a focus on Computational Fluid Dynamics and Data-Driven Fluid Mechanics. Currently pursuing my M.Sc. degree under the supervision of Dr. Majid Siavashi, I am passionate about bridging traditional fluid mechanics with cutting-edge machine learning techniques, particularly Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) for complex multiphase flow simulations.

Research Interests

Multiphase Flow

Investigation of complex multiphase systems including gas-liquid flows, bubble dynamics, and interfacial phenomena in various engineering applications and porous media.

Physics-Informed Neural Networks

Developing PINN models for multiphase flow simulation, combining physics-based constraints with deep learning for accurate flow predictions in complex geometries.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Advanced CFD modeling using ANSYS Fluent, OpenFOAM, and custom solvers for complex flow phenomena including turbulence, boundary layers, and multiphase systems.

Data-Driven Fluid Mechanics

Integration of machine learning techniques with fluid mechanics, utilizing scientific computing and AI for enhanced flow prediction and optimization in engineering applications.

Transport in Porous Media

Investigation of flow and transport phenomena in porous structures with applications in energy storage, filtration, and reservoir engineering using both experimental and numerical approaches.

Scientific Computing & Machine Learning

Development of computational algorithms and machine learning models for solving complex engineering problems, with focus on fluid mechanics and heat transfer applications.

Microfluidics & Nanofluids

Study of fluid behavior at microscales, nanofluid heat transfer enhancement, and microfluidic device design for biomedical and industrial applications.

Turbulence & Boundary Layers

Advanced turbulence modeling using RANS and LES approaches, boundary layer theory application, and flow separation analysis in complex geometries.
